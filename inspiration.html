Inspirational Poems


1. He stands alone, in quiet thought,
Above the streets, where days are caught.
A soft pink shirt, the breeze’s touch,
Moments pass, but don’t mean much.

Gazing out with eyes unseen,
At rooftops red and skies serene.
Perhaps he dreams, perhaps he waits,
While life moves fast beyond the gates.

Barefooted stance on patterned stone,
In city noise, yet all alone.
A silent pause, a breath, a view—
The world below, his soul anew.




2. In a quiet corner, calm and warm,
Where colors and patterns softly swarm,
He sits at ease, in gentle light,
Beneath a gaze serene and bright.

The Buddha watches with knowing eyes,
As stillness settles and moments rise.
A book half-opened, a lamp’s warm glow,
Hints of stories still left to know.

Surrounded by blooms, by art, by peace,
In this small world, all worries cease.
Here he finds a quiet grace,
A little haven, a resting place.




3. In a sea of purple bloom,
You stand, dispelling every gloom.
A splash of pink so bold and bright,
A beacon in the morning light.

The flowers reach, as if to share
The joy of sunlight in the air.
Petals soft in lavender hues,
Matching grace and subtle clues.

With gentle hands and calm repose,
You blend where nature softly grows.
Amid the branches, bright and free,
You are as vibrant as the tree.

A moment held, serene and pure,
Where beauty lingers, sweet and sure.
In fields of color, lush and grand,
Nature holds you in its hand.
